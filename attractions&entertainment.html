Top Attractions To Visit In Myrtle Beach

Broadway Grand Prix
1820 21st Ave N Myrtle Beach, SC 29577
843-839-4080

Broadway Grand Prix is a family-friendly attraction that features seven go-kart tracks, an indoor arcade, and mini-golf. The attraction also features a variety of food and beverage options. The price of this attraction differs depending on the activities you choose to do at the park. Pricing and other options available at this attraction are shown here: https://broadwaygrandprix.com/.



Myrtle Waves Water Park
3000 Mr. Joe White Ave Myrtle Beach, SC 29577
843-913-9250

Myrtle Waves is a family-friendly water park attraction in Myrtle Beach. It features roughly 30 slides and attractions (using 1.2 million gallons of water), along with food and beverage options for everyone. This attraction also offers retail items as well as different types of cabanas for rent daily. It is well known for its FlowRider attraction, which is the only one in North America, as well as its newest slide, the Wild Water Racer (which pays tribute to Wild Water and Wheels â€“ a go-kart and water park attraction that recently closed down in Surfside Beach). For pricing and options available at the park, visit https://myrtlewaves.com/.



SkyWheel Myrtle Beach
1110 N Ocean Blvd Myrtle Beach, SC 29577
843-839-9200

SkyWheel is a 200-foot LED observation wheel that is conveniently located on the Myrtle Beach Boardwalk. It is a fairly short walk from the beach, and it features a bar and grill on site for food/drink options. Each paid ticket on the wheel includes four revolutions with must see views of Myrtle Beach! For more information, please visit https://skywheelmb.com/.




Top Entertainment Options In Myrtle Beach

House of Blues Myrtle Beach
4640 Hwy 17 S North Myrtle Beach, SC 29582
843-272-3000

The House of Blues is a restaurant and live concert venue that features rock and blues style music, as well as various southern-style dishes. They host various events throughout the year and allow others to rent the venue for private/public events. For more information about the House of Blues, visit https://www.houseofblues.com/myrtlebeach/.



The Carolina Opry
8901 N Kings Hwy Myrtle Beach, SC 29572
800-843-6779

The Carolina Opry, owned by Calvin Gilmore, is an entertainment theater in Myrtle Beach that specializes in music, comedy, and dancing. It is also home to multiple productions and concerts throughout the year. More information regarding The Carolina Opry can be found at https://thecarolinaopry.com/.



Medieval Times
2904 Fantasy Way Myrtle Beach, SC 29579
888-935-6878

Medieval Times is a dinner theater in Myrtle Beach. It features multiple dinner options with a live tournament of jousting, sword fighting, and games of skill. It revolves around royalty by featuring a royal meal and a show for one price! It is an incredible experience, and more information can be found at https://www.medievaltimes.com.
