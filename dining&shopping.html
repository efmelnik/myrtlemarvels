Top Dining Options In Myrtle Beach

Riptydz Oceanfront Grill and Rooftop Bar
1210 N Ocean Blvd Myrtle Beach, SC 29577
843-945-1204

Riptydz is an oceanfront grill and bar that is located on the Myrtle Beach Boardwalk. They serve numerous food such as seafood, prime steaks, cocktails, and vegetarian dishes. They also feature live music as well. For information regarding their menu and pricing, visit http://www.riptydz.com/.



Rioz Brazilian Steakhouse
2920 Hollywood Drive Myrtle Beach, SC 29577
843-839-0777

Rioz Brazilian Steakhouse is a restaurant across from Broadway at The Beach that serves various meats, sides, and cocktails. They also feature a salad bar and multiple desert options, making this restaurant an ideal location to check out! Find more information about Rioz and their menu and pricing at this link: http://www.rioz.com/.



Captain George’s Seafood Restaurant
1401 29th Ave North Myrtle Beach, SC 29577
843-916-2278

Captain George’s Seafood Restaurant features a seafood-style all you can eat buffet that is sure to satisfy your cravings. From endless crab legs and juicy shimp to outstanding oysters and scallops, Captain Geoge’s Seafood Restaurant is sure to provide an exceptional seafood dining experience that you and others will enjoy. Not to mention, Captain George also offers various sides and steaks as well. For more information on menu and pricing for Captain George, visit https://www.captaingeorges.com/myrtle-beach-sc.




Top Shopping Options In Myrtle Beach

Tanger Outlets Hwy. 17 and Hwy. 501
10835 Kings Rd (Hwy 17) Myrtle Beach, SC 29572
843-449-0491

4635 Factory Stores Blvd (Hwy 501) Myrtle Beach, SC 29579
843-236-5100

Tanger Outlets (both Hwy. 17 and 501) are shopping malls with various brand-name and designer outlet stores scattered throughout. The Hwy. 17 shopping mall features a food court, as well as other dining options throughout the complex. The Hwy. 501 shopping mall, unfortunately, doesn’t feature a food court but does have dining options located off the property and close by. For more information regarding either of these shopping malls, please visit the following links for both: https://www.tanger.com/myrtlebeach17 or https://www.tanger.com/myrtlebeach501.



Coastal Grand Mall
2000 Coastal Grand Circle Myrtle Beach, SC 29577
843-839-9110

Coastal Grand Mall is a super-region shopping mall with a variety of dining and retail options in Myrtle Beach, SC. It features a Cinemark Theather attached to its food court and external shops and dining locations on the outskirt of its property. It is the second largest shopping mall in South Carolina. For more information about Coastal Grand Mall, visit https://www.coastalgrand.com/.



Broadway at The Beach and Barefoot Landing
1325 Celebrity Circle (Broadway) Myrtle Beach, SC 29577
843-444-3200

4898 Hwy 17 S (Barefoot) North Myrtle Beach, SC 29582
843-272-8349

Broadway at The Beach is a shopping complex located in the heart of Myrtle Beach, near other attractions and dining options. It features a plethora of stores and entertainment experiences for families, and also features fireworks throughout the week, with a special presentation for the 4th of July and New Year. Visit https://www.broadwayatthebeach.com/ to learn more about the attractions, dining, and shopping opportunities available at the property, as well as the firework schedule and scheduled events.


Similar to Broadway at the Beach, Barefoot Landing features entertainment experiences and stores for families of all ages. It also hosts events, fireworks, and even has an amphitheter on site. Finally, Barefoot Landing has dining options on site and views of the waterway to make the experience more memorable. Their website, https://www.bflanding.com/, features all of their stores, their schedule for events/fireworks, and dining/entertainment options on and around the property.
